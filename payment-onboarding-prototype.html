SEBI Regulatory Requirement

As a SEBI-regulated intermediary, you must obtain a validated UPI ID from SEBI portal before enabling UPI payments.

Why is this required?

SEBI circular (SEBI/HO/DEPA-II/DEPA-II_SRG/P/CIR/2025/86) mandates that all regulated intermediaries must use validated UPI IDs with @valid handles for payment collection to ensure compliance and security.

Important: While you can get a VPA from any bank on the SEBI portal, Razorpay currently only supports VPAs from **HDFC, ICICI, and Axis** banks.
